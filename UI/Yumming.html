Ensemble

Type: Italian

Pricing: Fair, tip a lot

Location: 850 Thurlow St.

Phone: 604-569-1770

Description: Dale MacKay, former Lumiere chef and Top Chef Canada winner shifted into bistro gear from his high-end, Lumiere days.
